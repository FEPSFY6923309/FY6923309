Abstract

      
From mapping out our earth to exploring deep space unmanned vehicles have both helped our everyday lives to great extents but also have brought great destruction and grief to our lives. In this article I will talk about the daily uses of unmanned vehicles in both civilian and military purposes bringing to light just how much we as humans require them for our day-to-day existence in our current state.
